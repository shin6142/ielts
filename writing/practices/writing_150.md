# Question
Some people think that robots are very important for future human development. Others, however, think that robots are dangerous inventions that chould have negative effects on society. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

# My Response

Some people mention that robots will play important roles to help human to live better in the future society. In contrast, others oppose this notion and state that robot evolution will impact on society negatively. In my opinion, the society can not progress without relying on robots capabilities.

Those who disagree with the idea that robots will enhance our society may point out the risk of unemployment because robots can automate manual works and make human labours redundant. For instance, in restraurants and supermarkets in modern cities, various robots are installed in order to automate a process such as washing dishes, managing payment and even cooking dishes. Those automation can be a cause of decrease in demands for human resources, which make it harder for human to find a job.

However, even though robots replace human resources with themselves, they bring numerous benefits to the society. Robots can enables people to live with ease. Nowadays, robots for house chores became common. By utilizing those type of robots, people can have more spare time so that they use their limited time more meaningfully. Moreover, robots also improve human's workforce. Robots are available as long as their battery last, which means that they can be exposed to long hour working or in dangerous situations. It will decrease the cruel accident and issues, such as mental probrem and injuries at workplaces.

In conclusion, there are some drawbacks of robots inventions while they can elimminates probrems in the current society. I personally stand by the idea that human should continue to invest and rely on robots for human well-being.
