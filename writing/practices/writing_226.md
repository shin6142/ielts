# Question
Business should put profit as their highest priority. 
To what extent do you agree or disagree with opinion and are there any other aspects that you think companies should value?

# My Response
Some people state making profit should be the top priority for business. Even though making profit is an essential activity to ran companies, I personally disagree with this idea becauce there are other aspects that business owners should treasure. 

First of all, companies should invest on human resources sufficiently. If they only aimed an increase in their profit, they would cut off benefits for employees. It causes their employees work less productively and look for other workplaces that they can get paid more. It is essential for any business owner to have employees to work at a comfortable working environment.

The second aspect that companies should care more than profits is their trustworthy in society. Even if their profit grow as they expected, once they commited to unfavored acts, such making money in an illegal way and treating stakeholders unfairly, they need to stop their business. This is the worst scenario that what employees dedicated are ruined.

Finally, companies are more responsible for the quality of their service. They should make their service entertain more people and make the majority of their customers repeatedly enjoy their service, which results in an increase in their revenue and profits eventually. If they only tried to raise their profit rates, they would invest less on their service operation or product development.

To sum up, I standby the idea that profit is not the first priority for companies since they should focus on investing on human resources sufficiently, being a trusted company and improving their customers satisfaction.
